It is possible to suggest a song to the list, albeit under strict guidelines. Please read the duidelines thoroughly. All submissions need to be sent by filling in the template form provided here and sent to us by email. Here a guide to the template:
  • Title:
  • Artist:
  • Year:
  • Duo: Specify here if the song is meant to be sung as a duet
  • Explicit: Specify here if the song contains explicit lyrics
  • Styles: Specify here one or more music-styles that apply to the song
  • "Whatness": Argument. as to why you think it qualifies as a whatsong. For help on that refer to our about page.
