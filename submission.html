It is possible to suggest a song to the list, albeit under strict guidelines. All submissions need to be sent by downloading and filling in the template form provided here. Send it to us by email with your song-file attached or provide a link (see below).

Understanding the template

What follows are explanations on how to fill in our submission template.

  • Full Name: Provide your full name
  • Email: Provide a working email adress
  • Title: The song title
  • Artist: The Artist or creator(s) of the song
  • Year: The year that the song was made
  • Duo: Specify here if the song is meant to be sung as a duet
  • Explicit: Specify here if the song contains explicit lyrics
  • Styles: Specify here one or more music-styles that apply to the song
  • "Whatness": Argument as to why you think it qualifies as a whatsong. For help refer to "What is a whatsong?" on our about page.
  • Internet-link: In case you are not able to attach the song as a file, please provide an online source in form of an internet-link. This may be a link for downloading the song (wetransfer, dropbox). Links to online music-platforms are welcome as well, provided one doesn't need an account to view them.
